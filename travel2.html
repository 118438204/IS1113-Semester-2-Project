Travel

Agadir, Morocco

Morocco is a country in the North of Africa. I visited Morocco in 2015 with my family. We stayed in Agadir, a coastal city to the south west of Marrakesh.

While in Agadir we went site-seeing, swimming at the beach and in the hotel pool, and visited the local markets called souqs.

Madrid, Spain

Madrid is the capital of Spain and is located in the centre of the country. I visited Madrid with my family in July of 2013.

We spent most days in the hotel pool as the city was going through a heat wave at the time! Madrid has two big soccer teams, Real Madrid and Atletico Madrid. We went on a tour of the Real Madrid stadium, the world famous Santiago Bernabeu.

Paris, France

Paris is the capital of France, I visited Paris with my secondary school in 2015. We stayed in a hotel near the center of Paris.

We visited all the major tourist attractions, including the Eiffel Tour, Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre museum.
