Sports

GAA

I have a keen interest in the Gaelic Athletic Association (GAA). I both play and watch football and I love watching Hurling. I have won numerous competitions with my local GAA club, including captaining the team to an U16 South Kerry Championship title. I support Kerry in both hurling and football. In the summer of 2017, I was involved in training the under 8 side for my GAA club. I have also done fundraising charity events with the club, including bag packing in the local Supervalu to raise money for Autism Ireland

Soccer

I love both watching and playing soccer. I support Arsenal Football Club in the Premier League and the Republic of Ireland in the Internationals. I played soccer for my Secondary School in both 5th and 6th year. I travel to Dublin to watch Ireland matches every summer and I go to London once a year to watch an Arsenal match in the Emirates stadium.

Basketball

From a young age I have had a great interest in basketball. I played basketball for a few years when I was younger with my local team, and I always played a year of basketball with my Secondary School in first year. I always really enjoy watching the NBA. I support the Indiana Pacers.
